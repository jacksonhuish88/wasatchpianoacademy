FAQ

Where are lessons held?

In the Wasatch Front area (Utah). Limited in‑home options available; online lessons by request.

What ages do you teach?

Generally ages 6 and up. Teens and adults welcome—beginners to advanced.

How does the free trial work?

We’ll do a 20–30 minute session to assess level, set goals, and map your first month. No pressure to enroll.

Do you offer recitals?

Yes—low‑pressure studio recitals and optional challenges to keep motivation high.
